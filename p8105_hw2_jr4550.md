#### Description of Problem 1 datasets

The pols_df dataset characterizes the the political party representation
of the number of Senators (sen_dem & sen_gop) and Representatives
(rep_gop & rep_dem), and the political party of the President (dem/gop)
in the United States from 1947-2015. The snp_df shows the S&P values of
each month from Dec 1950- July 2015. The unemployment_df shows the
unemployments rates in the US from Jan 1948-June2015.

#### Covid Price Comparison

``` r
  price_comparison = 
  rental_df %>%
  filter(date %in% as.Date(c("2020-01-31", "2021-01-31"))) %>%
  select(zipcode, borough, neighborhood, date, rental_price) %>%
  distinct(zipcode, date, .keep_all = TRUE) %>%
  pivot_wider(
    names_from = date,
    values_from = rental_price
  ) %>%
  rename(
    jan_2020 = `2020-01-31`,
    jan_2021 = `2021-01-31`
  ) %>%
  filter(!is.na(jan_2020), !is.na(jan_2021)) %>%
  mutate(
    price_change = jan_2021 - jan_2020,
    pct_change = (price_change / jan_2020) * 100
  ) %>%
  arrange(price_change) %>%
  slice_head(n = 10)
```
